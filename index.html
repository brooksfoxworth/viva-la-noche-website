About Us

Viva La Noche, a company of skilled chefs founded in 2024, delivers exceptional culinary experiences tailored to your specific needs. We pride ourselves on creating engaging events, from hands-on sushi workshops that allow guests to participate in the culinary process to luxurious on-site hibachi presentations.

Beyond these interactive options, we offer a full spectrum of catering services, all characterized by fresh ingredients, meticulous preparation, and a commitment to providing outstanding value.

Elevate Your Event with Exquisite Catering

From intimate gatherings to large-scale events across the Carolinas, Viva La Noche delivers exceptional catering services. Our focus on fresh ingredients and expert preparation ensures a memorable culinary experience for you and your guests.
